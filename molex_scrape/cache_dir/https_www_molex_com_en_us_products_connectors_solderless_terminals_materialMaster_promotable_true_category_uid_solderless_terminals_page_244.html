Solderless Terminals

A wide variety of reliable, high-quality solderless solutions that include terminals, splices, quick disconnects and wire management products that meet the interconnect needs of the electrical and industrial markets.

Contact Us
  1. Products
  2. Resources

Quick Disconnects

Providing fast and reliable connection and disconnection of two wires, fully insulated Quick-Disconnect Terminals are available in a variety of configurations including insulated, low insertion force and locking options suitable for a wide range of applications.

Find Parts

Ring and Spade Terminals

High-quality, seamless-barrel metric ring and spade terminals allow for optimum fit and maximum electrical performance when crimping with metric screws or threaded posts and are widely used in solderless wiring applications.

Find Parts

Splices

Splices for nearly every type of wiring need, from simple wire splices to splices designed for heavy-vibration applications and in harsh environments where sealed connections are required.

Find Parts

Battery Cable Lugs

Offer seamless performance connecting cables, electrical appliances and other power mechanisms, for harsh-duty environments.
